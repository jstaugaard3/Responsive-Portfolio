About Me

Joe is a Principal and Founder of Haverford Properties. Prior to establishing Haverford Properties, Joe was the Senior Vice President of Acquisitions for WP Realty, Inc., one of the largest retail real estate investors, developers and operators on the east coast. While at WP, Joe managed all of the business aspects of the company's transactions, including acquisitions, refinancing and dispositions, and managed the debt and equity sources for those transactions. He successfully closed over 120 transactions valued at more than $2,000,000,000. Prior to joining WP in 2004, Joe was a senior investment analyst at O'Neill Properties Group, a diversified real estate development company, where he managed acquisitions, refinancing and sales. Prior to beginning his professional career, Joe served in the United States Army for four years - attaining the rank of Captain. Joe received a B.S. in Computer Science from Shippensburg University, a M.S. in Finance from Virginia Commonwealth University and he is a Chartered Financial Analyst.​
